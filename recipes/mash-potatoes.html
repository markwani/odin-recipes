Make this recipe and everyone at the pot luck will love you. Skip the KFC drive through and make this instead!

Ingredients
  • Your choice of lay's(I like using Hot dill pickle)
  • Milk (preferrably expired)
  • Butter churned by my pet rat
  • Chives from Jack & the bean stalk
Steps
  1. Mash the chips and add butter to the mix
  2. Next add the milk and stir until good
  3. Add chives on top, serve & enjoy!
