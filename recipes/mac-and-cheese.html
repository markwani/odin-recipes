Mac & Cheese

Description

This a recipe to make homemade mac and cheese that will leave you full, but most importantly happy with how good it is. Make sure you acquire the needed ingredients listed below. This recipe is pretty simple only taking 10 minutes of preperations, and a cook time of 45 minutes for a total time of 55 minutes which isn't bad considering you can make big portions. So skip the box mac and cheese and follow the recipe down below!

Ingredients
  • Uncooked box of macaroni noodles
  • Butter and Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheddar and Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt, Pepper, & Paprika
  • Bread crumbs
    • Steps
    1. Boil the noodles, drain, & transfer to prepared baking dish
    2. Make the cheese sauce, pour over noodles, and stir
    3. Make the topping, spreading it over the mac and cheese, sprinkle with paprika
    4. Bake mac and cheese until golden brown
    5. Let it rest and Enjoy!
