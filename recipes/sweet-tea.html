Description

This is the sweet tea I was taught to made at the World famous McDowell's where the king of Zamunda worked here meeting the current queen of Zamunda. Make this recipe for a special someone and you'll for sure get engaged and maybe meet you Queen or King.

Ingredients
  • 10 Bags of Tea
  • 10 Gallons of Water
  • 15 Gallons of White Sugar
Steps
  1. Boil 10 and add the tea bags once it reaches a boil
  2. Add the Sugar and Stir until you're tired
  3. Serve with a lemon on the side & enjoy!
